The Peoples of Iran: Multiple Societies Under One Name

Can we speak of "the people of Iran" as a single whole — or is it more accurate to talk about "the peoples of Iran"? Different communities living under one name, each with its own experience, memory, fears, hopes, and relationship with the future.

This essay is still taking shape. If you have a question about "the peoples of Iran" that you think should be seen in this analysis — send it.
